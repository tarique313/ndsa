== Description ==
WR Mega Menu is a Powerful, Responsive, and User-Oriented WordPress menu plugin with a Dedicated, Intuitive menu builder. It gives you a complete control at designing and customizing your menu exactly the way you want.

<b>More detail:</b> <a href="http://www.woorockets.com/plugins/wr-megamenu/">http://www.woorockets.com/plugins/wr-megamenu</a>

<b>See demo:</b> <a href="http://demo.woorockets.com/plugins/wr-megamenu/">http://demo.woorockets.com/plugins/wr-megamenu</a>

<h4>Main Features:</h4>
* <b>Dedicated & Intuitive Menu Builder</b>: Our user-friendly menu builder will take away the hassles that come with Wordpress default menu configuration panel.
* <b>Fully Responsive</b>: All screen sizes and mobile devices are supported. A must-have feature for your menu in this golden age of mobile and big screens.
* <b>Profiles Control</b>: Create and save your menu settings into different profiles for later configuration or to apply on other themes.
* <b>Live Preview</b>: What you see is what you get. Track your changes immediately without switching back and forth.
* <b>Various Menu Icon Display Options</b>: You can choose to display your menu with text, icon or both.
* <b>Vertical and Horizontal Orientation</b>: the 2 most popular menu orientations are supported to fit your needs.
* <b>Drag & Drop Configuration</b>: create your desired menu with just little simple drag & drop steps.
* <b>Rich Mega Contents</b>: Picture, text, link and widget are all supported.
* <b>Custom CSS Supported</b>: the ultimate customization that let you change your menu style to be exactly the way you want.
* <b>Unlimited Colors</b>: change your menu background and hover to any colors.
* <b>Google Fonts Embedded</b>: choose or change your favorite Google Font easily from our plug-in dashboard.

1. Upload WR Mega Menu to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
